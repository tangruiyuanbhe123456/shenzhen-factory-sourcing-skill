# Shenzhen Factory Sourcing

## Finding Factories
- 1688.com (Chinese domestic wholesale)
- Alibaba.com (international)
- Canton Fair (twice yearly in Guangzhou)
- Huaqiangbei Electronics Market (Shenzhen)

## Factory Vetting
- Check business license (营业执照)
- Request factory audit photos/videos
- Minimum order quantity (MOQ) negotiation
- Sample quality check before bulk order
- Payment terms: 30% deposit, 70% after inspection

## Quality Control
- Pre-production sample approval
- During-production inspection
- Final random inspection (AQL 2.5 standard)
- Shipment container loading supervision
